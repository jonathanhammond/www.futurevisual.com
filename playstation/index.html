For the Players. Bringing big screen adventure and interactivity to mobile.

IMMERSE

Four friends, one adventure, YOU control the outcome.

Playstation wanted to bring an immersive storyline to mobile that would allow players to make choices through the film.

AMAZE

Players choices would influence the storyline and lead them into ever more bizarre scenarios as the characters followed the clues assigned to their respective Playstation symbols.

SIMULTANEOUS STREAMS

On top of the challenges involved in shooting a gorilla and zombies the final films had to be viewed simultaneously, so the viewer could see the effect their decisions were having on the storyline.

As the players exited from one scene they would appear in the next scene but in a different window as they influenced and moved through the action.
